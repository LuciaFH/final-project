ABOUT

"We strive to be an Institution"

We are FOLD, a 500 capacity club and home to state of the art creative studios. Based in the industrial heart of Canning Town, We show case art and music for all.

Run by founder Seb Glover and Lasha Jorjoliani aka DJ Voicedron, our 24-hour venue offers you the chance to never have to leave the party until you're ready to go.

We focus on bringing you the best artists, DJs and events so you can experience music in a brand new way. Our studio spaces offer you the opporunity to make your creative dreams a reality.

We run workshops for those looking to improve their production skills and hone their creative talents
